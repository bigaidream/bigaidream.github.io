Biography

Jie Fu is a happy 😄 machine learning postdoc with his human-friendly big AI dream. He is focusing on drug discovery (antibiotics discovery in particular) at Quebec Artificial Intelligence Institute (Mila), supervised by Yoshua Bengio and Chris Pal. He is also broadly interested in reinforcement learning and language processing.
